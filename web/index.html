Configure your macropad in the browser

Connect a PadKit over USB to live-edit key colors, brightness, lighting effects and what each key types — then save it to the pad's own flash. No install, no account, nothing leaves your machine.

Requires Chrome or Edge on desktop. Your browser will ask which device to share.

  • Keys — recolor each cap and remap its shortcut.
  • Lighting — brightness, effects and idle dimming.
  • Live monitor — press a key and watch it register.
