Browser flasher

Put PadKit on your pad.

Your browser writes the firmware straight to the chip over USB. Nothing is installed, nothing is uploaded, and the file being written is the one served from this page.

You need a small screwdriver, tweezers, and Chrome or Edge on a desktop.

Firmware image: reading

Rather use a terminal?

Build the patched isp55e0 flasher in the repository and run ./flasher/isp55e0 -f firmware/padkit.bin. Windows needs a one-time driver swap with Zadig for the bootloader whichever route you take, because the CH552 ROM presents a raw vendor interface. The flashing guide has the details.
